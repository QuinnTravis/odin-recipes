Kebab

A must try dish for meat lovers! A dish with many variations across the globe, however today we will be making Seekh Kebabs from my country Pakistan!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • onions
  • green chilies
  • fresh cilantro
  • fresh mint leaves
  • ground cumin
  • garam masala
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • red chilli powder
  • ground coriander
  • turmeric powder
  • black pepper powder
  • oil

Steps to make

  1. Prepare the meat mixture
  2. Marinate
  3. Shape the kebabs around skewers
  4. Cook the kebabs on the grill
  5. Serve :D
