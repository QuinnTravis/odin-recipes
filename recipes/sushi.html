Sushi

A sophisticated dish now made easy! Sushi originated in China, unlike popular belief that it came from Japan.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fresh fish
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger
  • Seaweed sheets

Steps to make

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Prepare the fillings
  3. Setup the rolling area
  4. Spread the rice on the sheets
  5. Add the fillings
  6. Roll the sushi
  7. Cut the sushi roll
  8. Serve :D
