Brownie

A favorite of all sweet tooths! Served with and without ice-cream, brownie is a must dry dessert

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Un-sweetened cocoa powder
  • Teaspoon salt
  • Baking Powder
  • chocolate chips(optional)
  • nuts(optional)

Steps to make

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Mix the wet ingredients
  4. Add the dry ingredients
  5. Bake
  6. Cool and Serve :D
